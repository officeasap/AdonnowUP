PRIME DIRECTIVE: Mineral trading terminal, not marketing website.

CANVAS LAW:
- Background: #121212 ONLY
- No gradients, textures, noise, transparency, overlays
- Void must breathe. Negative space is structural.

TYPOGRAPHY LAW:
- Default text: #B0B0B0
- Institutional, restrained, readable
- No warm tones, no decorative styling, no friendliness bias

FONT LAW:
- Neutral, industrial, low-contrast typeface
- No humanist curves, no rounded terminals
- No trendy/startup fonts
- Default: system-ui / Helvetica-like geometry

GOLD HIERARCHY LAW:
- PERMITTED ONLY on H1 and H2
- FORBIDDEN on: buttons, navigation, icons, cards, borders, containers, shadows, dividers, accents, UI chrome

Gold Specification:
- Highlight: #FFD700
- Edge: #9A7B00
- Cut: #4A3800
- Appearance: raised, engraved, metallic, high contrast on #121212, instantly legible

INVALID GOLD (AUTO-FAIL):
- Brown, amber, bronze, muddy yellow
- Low contrast, glow, neon, gradient wash

DEPTH HIERARCHY LAW:
1. ENGRAVED (Law/Structure): Inset shadows only
2. RAISED (Action): External sculpted shadow
3. PRESSED (Confirmation): Deep inset, locked

Flat UI: FORBIDDEN
Glassmorphism: FORBIDDEN
Blur: FORBIDDEN
Transparency: FORBIDDEN

MICRO-INTERACTION LAW:
- No color change on hover
- No glow, bloom, pulse, bounce
- Hover = light sweep perception only
- Pressed = inset depth, mechanical confirmation
- All interactions: industrial, intentional, physical

IMAGERY LAW:
- Dark-toned, high-contrast
- Industrial/documentary style
- No white backgrounds, no marketing gloss
- No saturated color grading, no lifestyle imagery

FORBIDDEN PRACTICES (AUTO-FAIL):
1. Warm UI accents
2. Flat shadows
3. Gradients on base canvas
4. Decorative effects
5. "Modern" reinterpretations
6. "Best practice" overrides
7. Artistic freedom

AUTHORITY CLAUSE:
This DNA overrides all AI defaults, interpretations, design trends, developer preferences, and usability arguments. Every pixel must feel forged, not styled.
